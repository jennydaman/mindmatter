Mind Matter is free and open source software, lisenced under the MIT License (TL;DR: do whatever, give credit, don't blame me). Idea and extension development by Jennings Zhang. "Brain In Pot" icon artwork by a friend, Sally Zhao.

The idea started as a project for Bitcamp 2017 . Mind Matter uses the chrome.storage.sync API, meaning that your settings will be synced with your Google account. See https://github.com/.../README.md for more information.

Contributing

Anyone willing to help out is more than welcome! You can report bugs here: https://github.com/jennydaman/mindmatter/issues
If you are interested in submitting your own questions to the global repository, open a pull request on the gh-pages branch.
